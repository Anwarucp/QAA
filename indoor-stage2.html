QAA INDOOR STAGE 2 OPEN ARCHERY CHAMPIONSHIP 2022.

At University Of Karachi Gymnasium.

Glimpses Of QAA Indoor Stage 1.

As promised, we welcome you back to the Indoor Stage of Archery in Pakistan. We are very happy to announce that QAA Indoor Stage 2 Open Archery Championship 2022 is being held at University of Karachi. The championship will held on Sunday, 21st August 2022, in New Gymnasium Hall. Registrations are Open for All Pakistan. You can participate in following categories: 10-meter Junior Archers 18-meter Pro-Junior Archers 18-meter Senior Archers Medals, Trophies & Cash Prize will be distributed among all winners. All archers are welcomed to participate in the greatest event of Archery in Pakistan. We will be seeing you on the Indoor Stage very soon!.
