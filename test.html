About us

QAA ARCHERY Development & Welfare Association is working for past decade in building an efficient archery platform to help build international standard training in archery a possibility. Started in 2012, we have established international level training for coaches and archers to be able to train, and become top archers. We are a promising archery development & welfare association. We provide support for Archery Clubs and Academies & organize archery Events at domestic and national level. We organize international standard archery events which are acclaimed nationally and internationally. We also provide archery equipment & accessories with specialized training and guidance related to the Archery Sport.

Mission And Vision

We aim to promote archery on grassroot level among young and ever growing individuals to indulge in the activity that archery provies to improve & build chahracter by participating. Archery improves focus, builds character and improves decision making capability which helps individual in real life scenarios and it can last forever.

Our mission is:

To empower its members to make archery a leading sport in Pakistan.

To build and regulate archery on practice ground and in-field competitions.

To provide awareness of and engage it’s members with the sport.

Archery Training Centres:

Provide Beginner and Professional Level archery training and prepare them for national and international standard archery shooting to be able to compete in world archery and olympic competitions.

Coaches Training Workshops:

Train Coaches and Provide best standards of coaches and technically advance training centers to be able for coaches and atheletes to compete at international level and provide best possible solutions to arhcers and coaches

Our Role:

Our basic and Fundamental role is to provide a platform for people to practice archery in fail safe environment through a program of efficient trainers, coaches and workshops to promote archer yon grassroot level meanwhile working on training professional archers to prove themselves on grandeur stages.

This will not only help us in learning to improve archery in Pakistan but to provide all the basic and advance level methods of archery training, equip archers and aware them of ever growing sport of archery Our Roles are further devided into three categories which are explained in details as following:

Archery Promotional Campaign:

We Provide support in archery by facilitating archers with basic archery training and equipment to initiate archery in youth and the elderly. By Providing Basic requirements in archery, People can easily take part in playing archery and gain it’s benefits that’ll help them groom themselves for their future goals and ambitions and plan ahead

Archery for Beginners:

By participating in basic archery courses and training program, people can learn archery and take part in archery competitions. We will be providing basic archery W facilities such as archery club, archery range with certified coaches. Will will also provide basic archery equipment such as bow, arrows and safety for training at beginner level

Professional Archery Facility:

We provide certified coaches & Advance level training for professional archers who are performing at National and International Platforms. We also provide support program W for archers related to Archery according to professional and world archery standard with latest and up to date archery equipment.

Archery Competitions and Facilitations:

Provide international standards in archery competitions and to facilitate others in organzinig archery competitions, provide olympic level players and officials to raise the standard of archery for the sports enthusiasts

Objectives:

To ensure safe and proficient enviroment for the development of archers and archery in Pakistan

Ensure archery development

Produce efficient training centers for development of archery players, coaches and officials

Reaching world standards

Engage in world standards of archery in Pakistan and make it easy for the athletes to develop and reach their full potential.
